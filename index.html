A Working Princess

When you ask a kindergartener what they want to be when they grow up, one of the answers may be "princess." While some may think this response is silly, that dream could become reality.
Meet Carmen Pangelinan. She is a real life princess and children’s entertainer, who dresses up and performs at birthday parties and events. Explore what Pangelinan's life is like as a princess.
